Main Course

Week-wise Schedule

  • Week 1

    UNDERSTANDING THE FUNDAMENTALS- Learning the fundamental principles of cinematography, such as composition, lighting, camera angles, and movement.

  • Week 2

    ACCQUIRING TECHNICAL PROFICIENCY- Dive into the technical aspects of cinematography, including camera operation, exposure control, focus techniques, and using different types of lenses.

  • Week 3

    EXPLORE ARTISTIC EXPRESSION- Explore the creative aspects of cinematography, such as storytelling through visuals, mood setting, color theory, and creating visual style.

  • Week 4

    ADVANCED TECHNIQUES AND SPECIALIZATIONS Delve deeper into specialized areas of cinematography, such as aerial cinematography, underwater cinematography, time-lapse photography, or working with specific genres like documentary or narrative filmmaking.

  • Week 5

    PROJECT MAKING-Work down with the accquired skills and use your creativity to make a project
